# Adani Group: Capital Structure & Deleveraging Strategy

## Overview
This project analyzes whether Adani Group should sell stakes in its businesses to reduce debt and unlock shareholder value.

The analysis focuses on capital structure, valuation impact, and strategic alternatives, reflecting real-world investment banking decision-making.

---

## Key Question
Should Adani Group pursue stake sales as a deleveraging strategy?

---

## Key Insights
- High leverage negatively impacts valuation multiples
- Stake sales can reduce debt and improve investor confidence
- Deleveraging can lead to valuation re-rating (EV/EBITDA expansion)
- Strategic execution and timing are critical

---

## Analysis Includes
- Capital structure evaluation
- Stake sale vs alternative strategies (refinancing, asset monetization)
- Valuation impact analysis (EV/EBITDA expansion)
- Risk assessment

---

## Model Highlights
- Simulated stake sale: 15%
- Estimated capital raised used for debt reduction
- EV/EBITDA multiple expansion from 8.0x to 9.5x
